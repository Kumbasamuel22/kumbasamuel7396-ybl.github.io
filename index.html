About

Who I am

I like to turn ideas into 3D shapes, visual effects, and simple stories on screen.

I’m a 3D artist and game designer with basic to intermediate knowledge in Maya, Maya FX, Unreal Engine, Unity, After Effects, and Premiere Pro. I enjoy building scenes, FX, and edits that feel smooth and readable.


My focus is on clear forms, strong silhouettes, simple but effective lighting, and FX that support the scene. I am still learning and improving, project by project, and I like combining game tools with video tools to create small presentations.
